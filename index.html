Chapter I

The Genesis

Born in Jaipur — the Pink City, where ancient forts whisper of empires built with vision and will — Mohit Kumar Khandelwal chose a different kind of architecture. Not of stone, but of data, logic, and enterprise systems.

In the early 2000s, the ERP universe was vast, uncharted, and ruthlessly complex. Where others saw chaos, he saw patterns. Where enterprises saw impossible migrations, he saw pathways.

Seventeen years later, his name is written across multi-country transformation programs, innovation awards, and architectures that power enterprises spanning continents. This is not just a career — this is a universe story.
